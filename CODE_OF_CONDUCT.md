# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to building an open, inclusive, and respectful community.

## Expected Behavior

1. Be respectful and constructive.
2. Focus on technical facts and collaboration.
3. Accept feedback professionally and help others improve.

## Unacceptable Behavior

1. Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, or hate speech.
2. Publishing private information without consent.
3. Repeated disruptive behavior in issues, PRs, or discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, lock threads, or restrict participation for violations.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues privately to repository maintainers.
